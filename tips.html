JS9 TIPS

Scale z1/z2

Open an image, then click on "Scale->set limits to zscale z1/z2".

RGB images

Open 3 FITS images, corresponding to red, green and blue map. On the "File" menu, select the red image, then click on "Color->red". Then select the green image on the "File" menu, and click on "Color->green". Then select the blue image and click on "Color->blue". Finally click on "color->RGB mode".
